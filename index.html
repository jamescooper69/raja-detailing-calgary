Detailing Services Built for Every Need

Interior Detailing

Deep clean every surface inside your vehicle — seats, carpets, dashboard, door panels, and more. We remove stains, pet hair, and odours for a completely refreshed cabin.

  • Vacuum & steam clean carpets and upholstery
  • Dashboard and console wipe-down
  • Door panels and trim cleaning
  • Window interior polish

Exterior Detailing

Restore your car's shine from the outside in. Hand wash, clay bar, polish, and protective wax — every panel treated with precision care to bring back that factory finish.
