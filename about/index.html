Our Story

Built on a Passion for Clean Cars

Raja & Co AutoDetailing was born from a simple belief — every driver deserves a clean, well-maintained vehicle without the hassle of traditional car washes or overpriced shops.

Founded right here in Calgary, we started as a one-person mobile operation with a pressure washer, a set of professional-grade products, and an obsession with doing things properly. Word spread quickly. Clients kept coming back — not just for the results, but for the experience: professional, punctual, and genuinely caring about your car.

Today we serve hundreds of clients across Calgary and surrounding areas, offering interior, exterior, and complete auto detailing packages. Our team has grown, our equipment has improved, but the commitment behind every job remains exactly the same.

Our Mission

To Make Professional Car Care Accessible to Every Driver

We believe convenience and quality should go together. Our mission is to remove the friction from car maintenance — bringing expert-level detailing directly to your home, workplace, or anywhere life takes you, at prices that make sense.
