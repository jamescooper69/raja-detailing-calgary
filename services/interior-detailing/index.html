Interior Detailing

A Cabin That Feels Brand New

Most people spend more time inside their car than they realize. Dirt, bacteria, odours, and stains build up gradually until your interior just doesn't feel clean anymore — even after a regular car wash.

Our interior detailing service goes far beyond a vacuum and wipe-down. We use professional-grade steam cleaners, fabric shampoos, and detail brushes to reach every corner, seam, and surface. The result is an interior that looks and smells completely refreshed.

Whether your car has pet hair everywhere, coffee stains on the seats, or just years of everyday grime — we handle it all with care and precision.

What's Included

Everything We Do Inside Your Vehicle

Every interior detail — regardless of package — follows our thorough multi-step process covering the entire cabin.

Floor & Carpet Vacuuming

Deep vacuum of all carpets, floor mats, and under-seat areas using commercial-grade equipment.

Seat Cleaning & Shampoo

Fabric seats shampooed and extracted. Leather seats cleaned and conditioned to prevent cracking.

Dashboard & Console Wipe-Down

All hard surfaces cleaned and treated with UV protectant to prevent fading and cracking.

Door Panels & Pockets

Door cards, handles, and storage pockets wiped down and cleaned of embedded grime.

Interior Window Cleaning

All glass surfaces cleaned streak-free with professional glass cleaner for maximum clarity.

Air Vent Cleaning

Dust and build-up removed from all air vents using detail brushes and compressed air.

Odour Elimination

Deodorizing treatment applied to neutralize smoke, pet, food, and general musty odours.

Final Inspection

We walk around with you to ensure every area meets our standard before we consider the job complete.
