Exterior Detailing

Restore Your Car's Factory Shine

Calgary's roads, salt, and weather take a toll on your vehicle's paint and finish. Regular exterior detailing isn't just about looks — it's about protecting your investment from the elements that work against it every day.

Our exterior detailing service goes far beyond a standard car wash. We use a multi-stage process: hand wash, iron decontamination, clay bar treatment, machine polish, and a protective wax or sealant coat — leaving every panel glossy, protected, and free of contaminants.

From paint swirls to road film, we restore your vehicle's exterior to a level that turns heads and protects your paint for months to come.

What's Included

Our Complete Exterior Process

Every exterior detail follows our proven multi-stage process to safely and thoroughly clean, decontaminate, and protect your vehicle's paint.

Pre-Wash Rinse

High-pressure rinse to remove loose dirt and debris before any contact washing — preventing scratches from particles dragged across paint.

Two-Bucket Hand Wash

Safe two-bucket method using pH-neutral, paint-safe shampoo to lift and remove all surface contamination without scratching.

Clay Bar Decontamination

Clay bar treatment removes embedded contaminants — industrial fallout, tar, tree sap — that washing alone cannot reach.

Paint Polish

Machine polish corrects minor swirl marks, light scratches, and water spots — restoring depth and clarity to your paint's finish.

Protective Wax or Sealant

A quality carnauba wax or polymer sealant applied to protect your paint from UV, water, and environmental damage for months.

Tyre & Rim Cleaning

Wheels decontaminated, brake dust removed, tyres cleaned and dressed with a rich, lasting tyre shine product.

Trim & Plastic Dressing

All exterior black plastics and rubber trim restored with UV-resistant dressing to prevent fading and cracking.

Exterior Window Polish

All exterior glass cleaned and polished streak-free — improved visibility and a crisp, finished look from the outside.
