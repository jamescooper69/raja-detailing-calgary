Complete Detailing

Inside Out — The Ultimate Vehicle Transformation

Our complete auto detailing package is the most thorough service we offer. It combines our full interior deep clean with our comprehensive exterior treatment — leaving your vehicle looking, feeling, and smelling like it just rolled off the showroom floor.

This is the package people choose when they want the best possible result: before selling a vehicle, after buying a used one, before a special event, or simply because their car deserves a proper reset.

Every surface, every panel, every seam — treated with precision, professional products, and genuine care. This is complete auto detailing done right.

What's Included

Everything — Inside and Out

The complete package merges our full interior and exterior processes into one comprehensive treatment covering your entire vehicle.

Full Interior Deep Clean

Complete vacuum, shampoo, steam clean, and treatment of all interior surfaces — seats, carpets, dash, door panels, and vents.

Exterior Hand Wash & Decontamination

Pre-wash, pH-neutral hand wash, iron decontamination, and clay bar treatment to fully prepare the paint surface.

Paint Polish & Protection

Machine polish to remove swirls and light scratches, followed by a quality sealant or wax to protect the finish for months.

Tyre, Rim & Trim Treatment

Wheels fully decontaminated and polished, tyres dressed, all exterior plastics and rubber trim restored.

Engine Bay Wipe-Down

The engine bay cleaned and degreased for a complete and presentable vehicle from every angle.

All Glass Cleaned

Interior and exterior glass polished streak-free — every window and mirror treated for perfect clarity.

Odour Elimination Treatment

Deodorizing treatment applied throughout the cabin to neutralize any odours from pets, food, smoke, or general use.

Full Walkthrough Inspection

We walk through the entire vehicle with you on completion — inside and out — to ensure everything meets our standard.
