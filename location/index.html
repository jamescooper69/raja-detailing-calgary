FAQs

Location & Service Area Questions

Common questions about where we operate, how mobile service works, and what to expect.

Do you come to my home or do I need to drop the car off?+
We come to you. Our service is fully mobile — we bring all the equipment, water, and products we need directly to your location. Whether it's your driveway, your workplace parking lot, or anywhere else in Calgary, just give us the address and we'll be there.
Do you charge a travel fee to come to my location?+
There is no travel fee for any location within Calgary city limits. For locations outside Calgary — such as Airdrie, Chestermere, Cochrane, Okotoks, or other surrounding towns — a small travel fee may apply depending on the distance. We'll always confirm this when you book so there are no surprises.
What do I need to provide at my location?+
For most services, nothing at all — we're completely self-contained. However, for services requiring a water source (such as full exterior washes), access to a standard outdoor tap can be helpful, though not always required. We'll let you know in advance if anything specific is needed for your appointment.
Can you detail my car at my workplace?+
Absolutely. Many of our clients prefer to have their car detailed while they're at the office. Just make sure there's a parking spot available for us to work around the vehicle. We're discreet, professional, and fully self-contained.
Do you service areas outside Calgary?+
Yes — we regularly service Airdrie, Chestermere, Cochrane, Okotoks, High River, and Strathmore. For towns further out, please call us and we'll let you know if we can accommodate you. A travel fee may apply for locations outside the city.
